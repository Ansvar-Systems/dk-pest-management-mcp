# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides pest management data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional pest management, agronomic, or pesticide application advice. Treatment recommendations, product approvals, and IPM thresholds are based on published Danish sources (Middeldatabasen, SEGES Innovation, Aarhus Universitet, Miljostyrelsen), but individual field conditions, resistance profiles, and local regulations vary. Always consult a qualified plant protection advisor (planteavlskonsulent) before making crop protection decisions.

## Pesticide Data

Pesticide approval data is sourced from Middeldatabasen (middeldatabasen.dk). **Always check the current Middeldatabasen for the most up-to-date approvals and conditions before purchasing or applying any pesticide product.** Product approvals can be revoked or amended at any time. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Pesticide use in Denmark requires a valid spray certificate (sprojtecertifikat).

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from:

- **Middeldatabasen** -- Aarhus Universitet / Miljostyrelsen
- **SEGES Innovation** -- Landbrug & Fodevarer
- **Aarhus Universitet** -- Institut for Agroekologi
- **Miljostyrelsen** -- Danish Environmental Protection Agency

Data is publicly available from Danish government and agricultural research institutions.

## Currency

Pest management data changes. Product approvals are amended regularly. Resistance profiles shift over seasons. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
